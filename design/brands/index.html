Brands

BacklotCars

The BacklotCars brand is configured as a logo based on the Mark Pro typeface, a sans serif typeface that enhances the values of innovation, technology and modernity that we want to associate with the brand. The sans serif also conveys the values of rationality and balance associated with engineering.

Main graphic brand

This page reproduces the brand in its corporate color, with which it should be represented whenever possible. It also includes the model that must be followed in case it is necessary to reproduce the inverted corporate identity.

Float

Financing with Float offers you the feeling of lightness and assurance but also anticipation. You can live bolder, knowing Float has you covered. It’s an energizing brand.

The logo builds on from BacklotCars’ dynamism and, just like Transport, using the Mark Pro in italic to build recognition as sub brands — we imply movement and lightness with the O.

Where appropriate, the logo is animated on to emphasise the feeling of lightness

Transport

Transport is the combination of the speed of getting from A — B with the feeling of trust and freedom. This is what we intend to capture in our communication.

The logo builds on from BacklotCars’ dynamism, using the Mark Pro in italic. The angle of the italics becomes an own- able element that we can use to imply the speed of the service.

Where appropriate, the logo is animated on to emphasise the dynamism
